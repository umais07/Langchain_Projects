# 🎤 ComedyBot — Your AI Stand-Up Comedian

> *"Why did the chatbot tell a joke? Because it had great punchline-tuning!"*

ComedyBot is an AI-powered chatbot with a sense of humor. Ask it anything, and it'll respond with wit, timing, and (mostly) tasteful jokes. Whether you want a laugh, a pun, or a full stand-up routine, ComedyBot has you covered.

## ✨ Features

- 🤣 **Comedian Persona** — Responds in character as a witty stand-up comedian
- 💬 **Conversational** — Maintains context across the conversation for natural banter
- ⚡ **Instant Replies** — Fast responses so your jokes never miss the beat
- 🧠 **Powered by Groq** — Uses Groq under the hood
